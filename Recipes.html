Brownie Recipe!

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour

    • Instructions

  • Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
  • Line an 8 x 8 baking pan with aluminum foil and leave excess foil to hang over two opposing sides for easy removal.
  • Grease and flour bottom and sides of the foil; set aside.
  • In the medium bowl, melt butter in microwave on HIGH at 30 second intervals, stir and repeat until fully melted.
  • Whisk in sugar and cocoa. Put in eggs and vanilla.
  • Using a rubber spatula, fold in flour until flour is completely blended in cocoa. Pour evenly into pan.
  • Bake for 35 to 40 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. ENJOY!

Cookies Recipe!

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons purse vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (12 oz)

    • Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  • Line 3 baking sheets with parchment paper aside
  • In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set it aside.
  • Cream together butter and sugars until combined
  • Add egg and vanilla until light (about 1 minute)
  • Mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  • Add chocolate chips and mix well
  • Roll 2-3 tablespoons of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly on your prepared cookie sheets
  • Bake in preheated over for 8-10 minutes
  • Take them out and enjoy!

Funfetti Cake Recipe!

  • 3 3/4 cups of cake flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups of unsalted butter softened
  • 2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup of vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 3 teaspoons of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups of buttermilk, room temperature
  • 3/4 cup of rainbow sprinkles

    • Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  • Grease a cake pan with parchment paper rounds, then grease parchment paper
  • Whisk the flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Set Aside
  • Beat the butter and sugar together on high speed for 5 minutes until creamed together and fluffy
  • Add oil and beat for 1 minutes until combined
  • Add the eggs, egg whites, and vanilla extract and beat until combined
  • Add dry ingredients and buttermilk in three additions, mixing after each addition
  • Pour and spread the batter evenly into each cake pan
  • Bake for around 25 minutes or until the cake is baked enough
  • Take out the cake and let it cool down then enjoy!
